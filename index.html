Popular Music Albums

CHANGES

Changes is the fifth studio album by Canadian singer Justin Bieber. It was released through Def Jam Recordings and RBMG on February 14, 2020. Changes was supported by two US Billboard Hot 100 top-five singles: "Yummy" and "Intentions". "Get Me", which features Kehlani, was released as the sole promotional single.

PURPOSE

It was released through Def Jam Recordings and School Boy Records on November 13, 2015, as a follow-up to his third studio album Believe (2012).Beliebers will get an inside glimpse into their relationship when they listen to Justin's album, Purpose. He revealed that "What Do You Mean," a "little bit" of "Sorry," and a track titled "Mark My Words" were written about Selena, who he dated on and off from 2010 to 2013.

JUSTICE

Justice is the sixth studio album by Canadian singer Justin Bieber. It was released on March 19, 2021, by Def Jam Recordings. The album features guest appearances from Khalid, Chance the Rapper, the Kid Laroi, Dominic Fike, Daniel Caesar, Giveon, Beam, Burna Boy, and Benny Blanco. The album contains 16 tracks.
